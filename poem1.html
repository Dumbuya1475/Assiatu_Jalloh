    BAD DAY

    My mind is sick

    My mood is sad It has been a week And it's getting mad Tomorrow is Eid

    What's up with me?


    Nothing smells favourable

    All I sight is unpleasant Nothing match my mood

    The day become my enemy

    Whilst the night is uninviting


    I am lost in the mid of a

    desert where I couldn'

    differ the Cardinal

    directions


    I am hopeless With thoughtless actions.

    Is this depression or what?
